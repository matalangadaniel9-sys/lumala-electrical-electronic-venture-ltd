About Us

Lumala Electrical & Electronic venture LTD was established in 2008 to address the customer requirement for electrical, ELECTRICAL AND STRUCTURAL CABLING WORKS. We pursue our passion in design, solution, supply & service requirements for various industries like Steel, Cement, Plastic, domestic use, and many more. We also provide services for consultancy, design, installation, maintenance, and repairs of industrial and domestic electrical and control products.

We possess more than 10 years of experience and skilled resources to address the customers’ concerned areas of our field of expertise relating to international standards, quality, trust, and cost-effective solutions.

Our Products

Overview

We provide complete electrical installation and structured cabling solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial environments. Our services are designed to meet international electrical codes, safety standards, and instrumentation requirements, ensuring reliable power distribution and accurate signal transmission.

Our shop specializes in the supply, installation, testing, and commissioning of electrical systems and low-current infrastructure that support modern automation, communication, and measurement systems.

Electrical Works Services

We offer professional electrical services, including:

  • Power Distribution Systems
    • Main & sub-distribution boards
    • Circuit breakers, isolators, and protection devices
    • Cable sizing and load calculations
  • Industrial & Commercial Wiring
    • Conduit and trunking installations
    • Armoured and non-armoured cable laying
    • Earthing and lightning protection systems
  • Instrumentation Power Supply
    • 24V DC and AC power for transmitters and controllers
    • Panel wiring for PLCs, relays, and control systems
    • Signal and power segregation to reduce interference

Testing & Commissioning

  • Insulation resistance testing
  • Continuity and polarity testing
  • Voltage, current, and earth resistance measurements

Structured Cabling Works

We design and install structured cabling systems that ensure high-quality signal integrity and future scalability.

  • Data & Communication Cabling
    • Cat6 / Cat6A / Fiber Optic installations
    • Network racks, patch panels, and cable management
    • LAN, WAN, and Internet infrastructure
  • Instrumentation & Control Cabling
    • Control cables for sensors and transmitters
    • Signal cabling for 4–20mA, HART, Modbus, and digital I/O
    • Shielded and twisted pair cable installations

ELV Systems

  • CCTV cabling
  • Access control systems
  • Fire alarm and BMS cabling
